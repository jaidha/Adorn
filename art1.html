Flow-Seattle

I have been working on some of the different design elements of Portfolio Volume 4 and this week I am focusing on the cover design. I wrote a post a few years ago on the cover design for Portfolio Volume 3 seen here. Not much has changed with the way that I am approaching this new cover. It's hard for me to accept the first or second design I come up with no matter how much I am in love with it. I instead force myself to test out several ideas as a way to keep the design process a little more relaxed and free flowing.
